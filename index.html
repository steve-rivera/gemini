Frequently Asked Questions

How do the credit card rewards work?

With the extra20SM credit card , you can get paid for your everday spending. You spend $400, we pay you $10 per month. Then, you get another $10 per month when you spend another $400. That’s a total of $20 a month that we credit back to you.

Is this just a one-time promotion?

This will not be a one-time promotion. We plan to give a monthly bonus for our extra20SM credit card customers. There’s no cap on how long you can collect your $20 per month as long as you qualify. The bonus is credited directly to your monthly bill.
